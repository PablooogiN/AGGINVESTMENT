You may look up any stock, currency exchanges, and related information through news articles provided by The New York Times. Begin by clicking on the stock icon and search a stock ticker to retrieve its graph. You may view daily or long-term stock charts. Click on the newspaper icon to select from a series of articles to read about. Click on the currency exchange icon to retrieve its graph and compare currencies to each other. The list icons keep track of your visited stocks, currencies, and investments by adding and removing.

Drag the title bar to move the windows or resize them from the bottom right corner.

If you are lost, just refresh the page!
